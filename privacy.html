Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Data Collection and Local Storage

peakloads ("we", "us", or "our") operates the peakloads powerlifting calculator application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our application.

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers. peakloads is designed as a privacy-first, offline-capable application. Any data you enter (such as weights, sets, reps, or calculator settings) is saved exclusively in your browser's local storage (LocalStorage) on your device.

Purpose of Local Storage

We use LocalStorage strictly for the purpose of improving your user experience. It allows the application to "remember" your inputs and settings across sessions, so you can close the browser and resume your calculations without re-entering data. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email or contact information, it is your responsibility to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
